About Bosco Dance Company

Transforming Dreams into Unforgettable Experiences Worldwide.

Bosco Dance Company, based in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, is a premier destination for world-class event planning and choreography. Founded in 2006 by Bosco(David), a Mumbai native with a passion for dance, the company has become synonymous with unforgettable celebrations, especially in the realm of weddings and sangeet ceremonies. With a strategic location behind Taj Krishna, Bosco Dance Company is known for exceeding client expectations, thanks to its team of dedicated professionals.

Under Bosco's visionary leadership, the company has grown into one of South India's most successful dance establishments. Bosco's early influences from renowned choreographers like Pappu Mallu and Ganesh Hegde in Mumbai have shaped his unique approach to dance. As a leading Sangeet choreographer, Bosco's expertise in creating innovative dance themes has made him a trailblazer, with over 3000+ choreographed shows to his credit, making each event a memorable experience.
