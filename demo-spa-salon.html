About Bosco Dance Company

Bosco: Master of Dance Innovation

Bosco, a visionary from Mumbai, founded the esteemed Bosco Dance Company in 2006. As a trailblazer in South India's dance scene, his extraordinary choreography and creative brilliance have redefined performances and captivated audiences. With a foundation shaped by renowned mentors and a remarkable portfolio of over 3000 shows, Bosco continues to enchant with his innovative dance themes, especially in Sangeet choreography, making him a leading force in the dance community.
